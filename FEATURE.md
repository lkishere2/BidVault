# Bid Vault Features Overview

Welcome to **Bid Vault**, a premium real-time auction platform designed to provide a seamless, secure, and exciting bidding experience. Below is an overview of the key features available to our users.

## 🔐 Secure & Easy Access
* **Google Quick Login:** Sign up or log in instantly using your Google account with just one click.
* **Email Registration:** Prefer using an email? We use a secure email verification code system to ensure your account is protected from the start.
* **Password Recovery:** If you forget your password, you can easily request a secure reset code sent straight to your email.

## 👤 Personalized Profiles
* **Profile Customization:** Update your profile picture (avatar), username, and password at any time to keep your account personal and secure.
* **My Storage:** A private inventory space where you can add and manage items you plan to sell.

## 💰 Wallet & Funds Management
* **Secure Transactions:** Manage your bidding funds directly from your profile.
* **Deposits & Withdrawals:** Request to deposit funds to start bidding, or withdraw your earnings from successful sales. 
* **Admin Verification:** To ensure the highest level of financial security, all monetary transactions are reviewed and approved by our administrative team before taking effect.

## 🔨 Selling & Hosting Auctions
* **Launch Auctions:** Select an item from your Storage and launch a live auction. You control the starting price, the start time, and the end time.
* **Smart Bidding System:** The platform automatically calculates the minimum next bid for your item (+5% of the current price) to keep the auction moving profitably.
* **Automated Lifecycle:** You don't need to manually start or stop your auctions. Our system automatically opens them for bidding at your scheduled start time and closes them exactly when the time runs out.

## ⚡ Real-Time Bidding Experience
* **Live Auction Rooms:** Enter an auction and watch the bids update in real-time. You never have to refresh the page to see the latest price.
* **Instant Bidding:** Place your bids with zero delay. Our high-performance servers ensure your bid is registered the millisecond you click the button, guaranteeing fairness even during intense bidding wars.

## 🤝 Community & Social Features
* **Follow Sellers:** Find sellers you like and follow their profiles.
* **Follower Notifications:** Whenever a seller you follow launches a new auction, you'll be instantly notified so you never miss a deal.
* **Real-Time Alerts:** Receive instant in-app notifications for new followers, outbid warnings, and important system-wide announcements from the administrators.
